### Directions  
1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees
2. Grab a large bowl. Add the chicken, 3/4 of the enchilada sauce, greek yogurt, garlic powder, cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper and mix. It should be saucy, not soupy.
3. Roll the filling equally into your tortillas and place them side by side in a baking dish.
4. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over it and the cheese
5. Bake for 20-25 minutes.


### Macros (per 2 enchiladas)  
Calories: **350 kcal**  
Protein: **40 g**  
Carbohydrates: **27 g**  
Fat: **12 g**

Super solid proteins per calorie ratio here. Convenient for meal prepping too. If you're gonna freeze this, make sure you you wait for it to cool down first. Wrap 2 enchiladas in foil.

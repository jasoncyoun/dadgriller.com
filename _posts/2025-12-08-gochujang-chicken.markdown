### Directions  
1. Pat chicken dry and trim excess fat
2. Mix gochujang, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, sesame oil, and water in a bowl. Because of the honey, this marinade can be pretty thick so just do your best in mixing it well
3. Add the chicken and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes but ideally you want to do this overnight in the fridge.
4. Cook the mixture in a large pan and pour over 1/2 cup of rice per serving.  I added green onions because I'm fancy like that.

### Macros 
Calories: **340 kcal**  
Protein: **34 g**  
Carbohydrates: **29 g**  
Fat: **10 g**

If you don't mind the waiting time, this one is pretty easy to make. Solid proteins and you can replace the thighs with breast too for more proteins per calories.
